Leading pipes only:

| foo | bar | baz
| --- | --- | ---

| ofo | rba | bza

Trailing pipes only:

foo | bar | baz |
--- | --- | --- |
oof | rab | zab |
ofo | rba | bza |

Leading and trailing pipes:

| foo | bar | baz |
| --- | --- | --- |
| oof | rab | zab |
| ofo | rba | bza |

foo        |    bar      |    baz
---    | ---    |     ---
oof   |    rab  |    zab
ofo    | rba  |   bza

---|---|---
oof|rab|zab
ofo|rba|bza

| bar
